About Us

EFA India conducts free Medical and Injury Emergency workshops in and around Mumbai to help people proactively save lives.

Services

What We Do

We conduct free workshops and seminars about the importance of first aid. We focus on clearing common misconceptions and educating people in the skills required for proper first aid.

Free of Cost

The workshops conducted are totally free of cost and they generate tremendous goodwill for the employer among the staff as well as among customers. Other organisations charge substantially for a simliar kind of arrangement.

Physical Reach

Unlike most first aid training programs, we reach directly to your establishment, thus creating a comfortable and more efficient atmosphere for learning. People attending the workshop do not have to take a leave and can fit it in their schedule.

Busting Myths

The sessions are interactive which is very impotant in order to avoid risky practices and misconceptions. Participants' doubts are directly addressed, helping them understand why some traditional practices are counterproductive.

CPR Training

A practice dummy is used to teach participants the proper way of performing CPR on a patient, and also when and why it is required. Volunteers are given a chance to practice CPR in order to prepare them to help any Cardiac Arrest patient.
